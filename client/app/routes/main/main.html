Meet River, your personal care companion.








No complicated apps to learn.

Just a friend who likes to say hi from time to time.

Track

River helps you track things like mood and anxiety so you can understand your triggers and progress.


Remember

The more medications you take, the harder it is to remember which you need to take. River sends friendly reminders to keep you on schedule.


Solve

Everyone wants to stick to their treatment plan, but sometimes real life makes it difficult. When problems come up, River is there to help you discover how to make it work.
